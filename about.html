TRIFECTA BAKERY ORIGINS

The Trifecta Bakery was established in 2015 by a young entrepreneur based in West Midlands. Due to allergies, he found it difficult to find gluten-free and eggless cakes. Passionate about desserts he went about starting his own business, where his intellect, professional experience and aspirations let him to be the sole founder of The Trifecta Bakery.

Here at The Trifecta Bakery, success has mainly been down to the fact that we specialize in making only three main types of cakes - hence the name Trifecta. These are Milk Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough, Dark Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough and Milk Chocolate Chip Brownies. We offer variations of different chocolates within each of these ranges. Our expertise within these three cakes has made us more successful over the years with an ever increasing customer base.

We provide mouth-watering flavours with the ingredients that we use, ensuring that each cake is made to the highest of standards. With this in mind we provide the best gluten-free eggless vegetarian cakes.

As all our ingredients are sourced we cannot fully guarantee that they are 100% gluten-free or eggless, however every effort is made to ensure we strive to make them as perfectly as we can. We do not use nuts in the manufacturing process, but some of our cakes use nuts as a topping. For this reason we cannot guarantee 100% nut-free cakes. Our cakes contain no meat, alcohol or any animal products except milk and butter. They are widely acceptable to Sikhs, Hindus, Christians and Muslims.
